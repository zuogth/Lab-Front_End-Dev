tour travel

BAI DINH PAGODA - HOA LU ANCIENT CAPITAL - MUA CAVE - THAI VI TEMPLE

(Tour code: TNT-PV 01)

TRIP SCHEDULE

8:00 AM Guide and driver pick you up and depart to visit Bai Dinh Pagoda
8:30 AM Arrive at Bai Dinh Pagoda. Admire the biggest pagoda in Viet Nam and the centre of Buddhism in Vietnam Northen. Time to visit and admire the beauties of Vietnamese Buddhist Pagoda Style as well as the design and architecture.
10:30 AM Leave Bai Dinh Pagoda to go to Hoa Lu Ancient Capital
10:45 AM Arrive at Hoa Lu - the Ancient Capital of Viet Nam independent Country in 10th Century with the two temples worshiping Kings of Dinh and Early Le Dynasty.A Established by King Dinh Tien Hoang and last for 42 years though 3 first Dynasties Dinh - Early Le - Ly
12:00 AM Lunch at the local restaurant
13:00 PM Finish having lunch and the continue to visit Thai Vi Temple
13:20 PM Arrive in Thai Vi Temple. The one of most beautiful Temple in the North of Vietnam for worshiping Kings in Tran Dynasty of Vietnam in 13th century.
14:30 PM Depart for Mua Cave
14:45 PM Visit Mua Cave and enjoy the stories about Tran Kings in Tran Dynasty of Vietnam
15:00 PM Start hiking up to 500 steps Ngoa Long Mountain to admire the beauty of Ninh Binh panorama view at the wonderful view point of Ninh Binh/
16:00 PM Drive back in hotel and end tour.

inclusions

  • Pick-up/drop-off by air-conditioned car.
  • English speaking guide.
  • Vietnamese lunch.
  • Entrance fees and sight-seeing tickets.

exclusions

  • Tax and travel insurance
  • Tips and other expenses not mentioned above

price

  • 2 persons: US$ 75 /per person
  • From 3-4 people: US$ 66 /per person
  • From 5-10 people: US$ 52 /per person
